Estancia en el School of Computer Science and Technology of Tianjin University (China).
Desde: 2/12/2016 Hasta: 15/12/2016 (2 semanas)

Resumen de la estancia

Dr. Rubén Jesús García-Hernández stayed at the School of Computer Science and Technology of Tianjin University (China), from December 2nd to December 15th, 2016. During his stay, he worked within the computer graphics group led by Prof. Dr. Qing Xu. Rubén gave a talk titled "Realism, NPR, interaction, virtual reality, serious gaming and applications", describing his research, on December 7th. He also provided guidance to students regarding Virtual Reality programming. As a result, a prototype of plane trajectory visualization for HTC vive was developed.

Financiación

Esta estancia fue financiada parcialmente por el proyecto NOMAD y por los proyectos "First-class Universities" y "First-class disciplines", con número de referencia 0903071002 del Consejo de Estado de China.
